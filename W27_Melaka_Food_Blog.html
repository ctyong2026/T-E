Use the cards as a one-page itinerary. Click “Open Map” for directions, then choose based on mood: heritage meal, street snack, dessert, supper or café.

🥥

Cool-down stops

Cendol, coconut shake and The Daily Fix balance the heavier meals with dessert and drinks.
